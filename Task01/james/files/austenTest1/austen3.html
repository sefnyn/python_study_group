In Memory of Jane Austen

Celebrating the Achievements of an Extraordinary Woman

Miss Jane Austen's legacy lives on in the hearts and minds of all those who have been inspired by her incredible story. Her remarkable achievements on the track, her charitable works and her literary prowess have left an indelible mark on the world.

To commemorate her memory, a memorial was erected in her honor in London's Hyde Park. The monument is a bronze statue of Miss Austen, standing atop a marble base inscribed with her famous words, "Life seems but a quick succession of busy nothings."

Each year, on the anniversary of Miss Austen's death, a memorial race is held in her honor. The race is open to both men and women, and the winner receives a trophy in the shape of the statue of Miss Austen.

Miss Austen's legacy lives on, and serves as a reminder of the power of courage and determination.
