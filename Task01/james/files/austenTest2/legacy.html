Legacy

Today, Jane Austen’s legacy lives on in the hearts and minds of racers everywhere. Her courage and determination are an inspiration, and her passion for the sport continues to inspire future generations. We hope that this website will serve as a testament to her greatness, and a reminder of the impact she had – and continues to have – on the world of racing.

Though she has since retired, Jane Austen remains an icon and a role model for aspiring racers everywhere. She is a reminder of what is possible, of what can be achieved, through sheer determination and hard work. We hope that this website will serve as a fitting tribute to her incredible life and career, and that it will inspire future generations of racers to reach for the stars.
